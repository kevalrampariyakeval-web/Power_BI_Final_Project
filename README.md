# 🌟 Executive Summary

Businesses generate massive amounts of transactional data every day, but without proper analysis, valuable insights remain hidden.

The **Sales Intelligence Dashboard** was developed to provide decision-makers with a centralized view of sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and operational efficiency.

Using Power BI, DAX, and Power Query, this project converts complex sales data into interactive dashboards that help stakeholders monitor KPIs, identify opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

This project helps answer critical business questions:

✅ How much revenue is being generated?

✅ Which products contribute most to sales?

✅ Who are the highest-value customers?

✅ Which category drives business growth?

✅ What are the monthly sales trends?

✅ How effective is return management?

✅ Where should the business focus its efforts?
